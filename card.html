Mini Rex Bunny

Mini Rex rabbits can jump as high as three feet.

  • Scientific Name: Oryctolagus Cuniculus
  • Average Length: 10-12 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 8-12 years
  • Habitat: low grasslands and forrested areas

Mini Rex rabbits are small rabbits known for their velvety, dense fur. They come in many colors, although the most famous is broken black, which mixes black with white. Mini Rex rabbits are particularly popular due to their compact size, beautiful fur, and plushy undercoat.
